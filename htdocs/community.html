DEVELOPMENT

The development mailing list is available at Google Groups, at sqlalchemy-devel.

SVN Commits can be viewed via the timeline (rss feed) which also mails to the Commit List. Ticket changes are posted to Ticket List which also sends to the sqlalchemy-devel list.

SQLAlchemy has a buildbot master at http://buildbot.sqlalchemy.org/.
